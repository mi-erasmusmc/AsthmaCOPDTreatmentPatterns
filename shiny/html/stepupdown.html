Description

The pie charts categorize switching between treatments in step up / step down / switching / start or end of acute exacerbation / off label use / not conform / stopped according to two definitions:

  • GINA guidelines: narrow definition following the GINA guidelines (2020), very clean definition but leads to undefined switches due to heterogeneity of observational data.
  • Broad definition: broader definition developed by clinical experts in the field, categorizing all possible switches by defining drug class levels and subsequent patient treatment levels (sum of drug class levels received by a patient).
